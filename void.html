Sequence of Greif

My father told me he would die weekly for ~10 years. Then he did. This feels like a natural chronology of events in type, but my fading recolletion of his death and the four trips home I took on each side of it make me question the sequence of grief. Much of him died in those preceding 10 years. Did he pass on the first or second trip? Or was it something I decided happened? I recorded the last two interactions I ever had with my father. His memory of me had already died to some extent. I composed two pieces with these recordings, because it helped.
