About the Project

Interactive map to visualize oxygen transport trait data across collection sites in South America. Four bird species distributed widely across elevations, are repsresented (Zonotrichia capenisis, Troglodytes aedon, Tachuris rubrigastra, and Phleocryptes melanops). Donut charts show demographic and elevation information. Histograms show species counts and distributions of oxygen transport trait measurements.
